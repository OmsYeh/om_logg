How to use me and what am I for

Hello there,
This is Om-Logg, A place where you can log your activities, habits, daily tasks, reminders and much more.

To start logging just head on over to sign-up or login, if you are already logged in, head on over to the Logs page - you can get there by pressing on the 'Logs' button up top.

When we speak about reminders we're not referring to the regular phone reminders that we all have on our devices,
we're speaking about reminders as in memories, not necessarily memories like that of your dog licking your nose,
rather memories that are important and shouldn't forget for instance, when you last took medication or when you
last fed your puppy that licked your nose and all the similar memories that need to be logged.
